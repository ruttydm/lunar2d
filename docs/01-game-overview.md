# Lunar3D — Game Overview

## Elevator Pitch
Lunar3D is a **browser-based MMO .io game** where players pilot lunar landers on a realistic, round Moon with real orbital mechanics. Land safely on pads, fight other players with gravity-affected projectiles, upgrade your lander through a deep tech tree, and climb the daily leaderboard — all in a persistent, shared lunar world.

## Genre
- **MMO .io** — persistent world, drop-in/drop-out, free-for-all PvP
- **Hard simulation** — real gravity, orbital mechanics, momentum, fuel management
- **3D browser game** — no install, no account required

## Target Audience
- Casual space nerds
- Fans of KSP, Lunar Lander, and .io games
- Players who enjoy physics-based gameplay with PvP chaos

## Platform
- **Browser only** (desktop + mobile)
- No account required — enter a name and play
- Progress saved in `localStorage` with server-side verification to deter cheating

## Core Pillars
1. **Realistic Physics** — compressed-scale Moon with real gravity; players can achieve orbit, deorbit, and land
2. **Skill-Based Landing** — thrust management, fuel conservation, precision touchdowns
3. **Emergent PvP** — shooting, pushing, sabotage; all projectiles affected by gravity
4. **Progression** — upgrade tree, lander unlocks, daily leaderboard
5. **Open World** — GTA-style level activation; explore the Moon freely

## Key Differentiators
- Round planet with real gravity wells (not flat terrain)
- Orbital mechanics in a browser .io game
- Gravity-affected projectile combat
- Compressed-scale space that makes orbits achievable in minutes, not hours
- No grindwalls — jump in, fly, fight, land

## Monetization
- **Fully free** — no premium currency, no pay-to-win, no ads (for now)
